Fall Janesville Driving Tour

In a rural area like Janesville, WI, when wanting to spend a day outdoors there are limited but unique options that only the countryside can provide. Janesville’s motto is “Wisconsin’s Park Place”, and features 64 parks including boat launches, golf courses and nature trails. While many of the parks simply feature tennis courts or playgrounds, Janesville has great options for spending a day outdoors with a wide variety of sports, sight-seeing and kid friendly activities that are featured in this tour to provide a good sample of Janesville’s outdoor activity options. While most activities are available at least summer through fall, fall is a great time to not only get the best cool weather but to enjoy the full extent of rural sight-seeing with autumn colors. Due to how spread-out Janesville and it’s activities are, using a vehicle to get to each location would provide the most enjoyable experience for this tour
